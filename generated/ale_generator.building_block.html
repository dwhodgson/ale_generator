ale_generator.building_block¶

ale_generator.building_block(c: float, z: complex, theta: float) → complex¶

Draws a slit of capacity c attached at the angle theta

Computes the image of a point in the complex plane z under the map which takes the unit disc to the unit disc with a slit of capacity c attached at the angle theta (i.e. the point e^(i*`theta`)). This is done by rotating through theta, attaching a slit at the point 1, then rotating back again.

Parameters
cfloat

The logarithmic capacity of the slit.

zcomplex

The point at which to compute the image of the map.

thetafloat

The angle at which to attach the slit to the unit disc - should be in the range [-pi,pi].

Returns
complex

The image of the point z under the rotated slit map.
